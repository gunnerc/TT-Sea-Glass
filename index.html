Background

I love Jewelry!

I love the beach!

I love sea glass!

One day, as I was walking along the beach and collecting sea glass, God gave me the idea of making it into jewelry. I knew that finding each piece of glass was a gift from Him, and I knew that it was going to be a passion and a pleasure to find these treasures and turn them into something beautiful. I knew on the spot that I would never be able to keep any of the money, so I decided that absolutely all of the profits would go to charity.

Today, these profits are shared between two charities: my local SPCA, and the Fairy Godmother project (in honor of Ann Smith, who provided a lion share of the sea glass I am working with).
